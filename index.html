18/19 Season Highlights

July 2018

Cristiano Ronaldo joins Juventus Football Club

On July 2018, Cristiano Ronaldo signed a four-year contract with JuventusFC.

October 2018

Goal 400 scored in national leagues

20th of October of 2018 marked the day that Cristiano Ronaldo reached another landmark. On this day he became the first player to score 400 goals across Europe’s top five leagues.

January 2019

2018 Italian Super Cup

Cristiano Ronaldo won his first trophy for Juventus, after scoring the game-winning and only goal from a header against AC Milan.

March 2019

Champions League achievements

During the 18/19 season in Champions League, Ronaldo became the first player to reach 100 victories and 125 goals on the competition. The season was also marked by a legendary hat trick against Atlético Madrid.

May 2019

Serie A championship and Most Valuable Player of the competition

Cristiano Ronaldo helped Juventus to win the 8th Serie A in a row. Finishing his first Serie A campaign with 21 goals and 8 assists, Cristiano Ronaldo won also the Serie A Most Valuable Player

May 2019

Cristiano Ronaldo becomes the first player to win 3 major leagues

With this last title, Cristiano Ronaldo became the only player in history to win the three major leagues in the world. Premier League, La Liga, and Serie A.

June 2019

Portugal wins first-ever Nations League

After winning the Nations League title, Cristiano Ronaldo was the first player in history to conquer 10 UEFA trophies. He also became the first player to score in 10 consecutive international competitions and the athlete with more goals in any National team.
